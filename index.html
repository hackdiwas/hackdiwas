United University X Codify_Club

United University's Faculty of Computer Applications & Department of Computer Science and Enginnering is gearing up for an exciting and innovative event, "Hack Diwas" a two-day Hackathon that promises to bring together bright minds and creative thinkers under one roof.

Set to be held exclusively at United University, this hackathon aims to foster collaboration, problem-solving and technological advancements. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their coding skills, develop innovative solutions to real-world challenges and network with industry professionals and fellow enthusiasts.

With a focus on promoting innovation and creativity, Hack Diwas at United University is poised to be a dynamic and inspiring event that pushes the boundaries of technology and knowledge.

About Event

Held at United University Prayagraj, this hackathon provides the ideal setting for intensive collaboration and competition. Spanning 20 hours over two days, the event offers participants ample time to immerse themselves in technological exploration and innovation. Real-world challenges will be presented, encouraging students to leverage their skills and creativity to develop viable solutions.

Hackathon Theme

Problem Statements

Theme 1 _ Healthcare


A. Develop a solution to improve access to healthcare services for underserved communities.

B. Address the challenge of managing chronic diseases more effectively.

C. Improve patient engagement and adherence to treatment plans.

Theme 2 _ Modern tech


A. Develop innovative solutions to enhance user privacy and data security in online platforms.

B. Address the challenge of misinformation and fake news on social media platforms.

C. Improve collaboration and project management workflows for distributed teams using digital tools like GitHub.

D. Data security on online platforms.

Theme 3 _ Education


A. Enhance learning outcomes for students through innovative technologies.

B. Bridge the gap in access to quality education, especially in remote areas.

C. Foster lifelong learning and skills development for a rapidly evolving job market.

Theme 4 _ Environment


A. Tackle issues related to pollution and environmental degradation.

B. Promote sustainable practices and conservation of natural resources.

C. Enhance resilience to climate change impacts at the local or global level.

Theme 5 _ Smart Cities


A. Address urban mobility challenges and improve transportation systems.

B. Enhance public safety and emergency response capabilities in cities.

C. Promote efficient use of resources and infrastructure planning for growing urban populations.

Theme 6 _ Agriculture


A. Improve productivity and sustainability in agricultural practices.

B. Address food security challenges and reduce post-harvest losses.

C. Enhance access to markets and value chains for smallholder farmers.
